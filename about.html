About

About our Shop:

We’re here to add a little extra sweetness to your day. The Ice Cream Shop is a new way to order your favorite ice cream brands right to your front door. The Ice Cream Shop features fan-favorite products from beloved ice cream brands Ben & Jerry’s, Talenti, Magnum, Breyers and Good Humor. We hand make over 40 flavours of delicious small batch, slow churned gelato in our Hawkshead shop. Each day we spice it up and serve a mixture of 22 flavours to the lovely folk of the Lake District, UK. We also make some bangin’ sorbets and dairy free flavours too. We believe that no one should miss out on a scoop so we are GLUTEN FREE & VEGAN friendly - please ask a member of staff upon arrival what tasty treats you can have!

About our Manager:

The Little Ice Cream Shop is owned by one crazy young woman on a mission, Amy. Amy opened the The Little Ice Cream Shop in 2016 when she was 18 years old as part of her plan to save up enough money to move to London and train as an actor. Little did she know that she had accidentally created the beginning of an ice cream empire. Soon after opening word spread and The Little Ice Cream Shop in Hawkshead had queues out the door - people were going crazy for her ice cream!
